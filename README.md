## Video Surveillance Redundancy Planning for Business Continuity

Downtime is unacceptable in security infrastructure. **Video surveillance redundancy planning** ensures systems remain operational during hardware failures, network disruptions, or power outages.

Redundancy strategies may include backup storage, failover servers, redundant network paths, and secondary power sources. These measures protect against data loss and ensure continuous recording during critical events.

Resilient surveillance systems safeguard evidence integrity and maintain security visibility even under adverse conditions.
